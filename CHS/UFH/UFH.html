Underfloor Hot Water Heating

At UpTech Engineering, we specialize in providing efficient and comfortable hot water underfloor heating systems. Our underfloor heating solutions ensure an even distribution of warmth across your space, creating a cozy atmosphere while maximizing energy efficiency. Using advanced hot water technology, our systems are ideal for various settings, from residential homes to commercial spaces.

Our hot water underfloor heating systems are designed to be compatible with central heating boilers and other heating sources. The heated water circulates through a network of pipes installed beneath your flooring, gently radiating warmth upwards, maintaining a comfortable temperature without the need for bulky radiators. The system is perfect for both large open spaces and smaller, private rooms, providing consistent warmth in any area.

Technical Specifications:

  • Heating Source: Compatible with gas, electric, or renewable central heating systems.
  • Pipe Material: Cross-linked polyethylene (PEX) or multilayer pipes designed for high temperatures and durability.
  • Pipe Diameter: Typically 16mm, with customizable sizing options.
  • Insulation: High-quality insulation layers to ensure energy efficiency and heat retention.
  • Operating Temperature: Adjustable range, commonly between 30-50Â°C for optimal comfort.
  • Control System: Digital thermostats with room-by-room control options.
  • Installation Depth: Typically 50-70mm, including screed, pipe, and insulation layers.
  • Energy Efficiency: Up to 30% energy savings compared to traditional radiator systems.
