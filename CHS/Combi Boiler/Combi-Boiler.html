Combi Boiler Details:

  • Dual Functionality: The combi boiler provides both central heating and hot water in a single unit. It doesn’t require a separate tank for hot water, which saves space.
  • Instant Hot Water: Heats water instantly as soon as the tap is turned on. No waiting time for hot water, making it ideal for household use.
  • Compact Size: Combi boilers are typically small and compact, so they can easily be wall-mounted. Ideal for small homes and apartments.
  • Energy Efficiency: Since it only heats water as needed, no extra energy is wasted. Helps improve efficiency and reduce electricity bills.
  • Easy Installation and Maintenance: Easy to install, with no need for a separate tank or extensive piping. Maintenance is also simple due to minimal complexity.
  • Pressure: Provides good water pressure, effective for showers, etc. Maintains consistent water pressure.
  • Fuel Options: Combi boilers generally run on gas, electricity, or oil. Choose the boiler based on the type of fuel you use.
  • Installation Cost: While installation may be slightly costly, it helps save money in the long run. No need for extra tanks or piping, making installation simpler.
  • Eco-Friendly: Uses fuel efficiently, reducing carbon emissions. Causes less harm to the environment.

This is the basic information about combi boilers. If you need details on a specific model or more technical information, please contact us.

What is a Combination Boiler?

A combination boiler is a high efficiency hot water and central heating boiler. Often referred to as a ‘combi’ it runs on demand meaning it only heats up the water that you need to use. This type of boiler requires no water storage or cylinder, meaning the space required for your heating solution is kept to a minimum.
