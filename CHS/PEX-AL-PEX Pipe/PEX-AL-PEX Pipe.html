PEX-AL-PEX Pipe

At UpTech Engineering, we offer high-quality PEX-AL-PEX pipes for reliable, efficient hot water heating systems. These pipes combine the flexibility and durability of cross-linked polyethylene (PEX) with an aluminum core, providing the perfect solution for high-temperature and high-pressure heating applications.

The PEX-AL-PEX structure features three layers: an inner and outer layer of PEX bonded to a central aluminum core. This aluminum layer enhances the pipe’s stability and prevents expansion, ensuring it holds shape when bent and resists deformation. The inner PEX layers provide corrosion resistance and high thermal conductivity, making it ideal for hot water applications.

Technical Specifications

  • Material Composition: Cross-linked polyethylene (PEX) inner and outer layers, with an aluminum core for stability.
  • Pipe Diameter Options: Available in various diameters, typically ranging from 16mm to 32mm for flexibility in installation.
  • Temperature Range: Operating temperature up to 95°C, suitable for hot water heating systems.
  • Pressure Rating: Rated for up to 10 bar, ensuring durability under high pressure.
  • Thermal Expansion: Reduced due to the aluminum core, maintaining pipe shape and reducing stress on fittings.
  • Corrosion Resistance: PEX layers provide high resistance to corrosion, ensuring longevity and efficiency.
  • Ease of Installation: Flexible and easy to install, even around corners and tight spaces, reducing labor time and costs.
