Hot Water Storage Tank

Storage Tank for Hot Water Heating System and Domestic Use

At UpTech Engineering, we provide high-quality storage tanks that are designed not only for hot water heating systems but also for domestic use. Our storage tanks are built to maintain long-term performance and ensure a constant supply of hot water. Whether your purpose is for domestic use or you require storage that is compatible with central heating systems for commercial purposes, our tanks perfectly meet your needs.

We design our storage tanks to be powerful and energy-efficient, allowing users to consume less energy while benefiting from a prolonged supply of hot water. Our tanks are available in various sizes and materials and can be customized to meet your specific requirements. These tanks perform especially well in areas where a high water temperature is maintained, ensuring that you always have access to hot water.

Features

  • Capacity Options: We offer a range of capacities, from 50 liters to 1000 liters, with custom sizes available upon request to suit your specific needs.
  • Material: Our storage tanks are made from high-quality stainless steel or GI (galvanized iron), making them durable, strong, and resistant to corrosion.
  • Insulation: Each tank is equipped with high-density polyurethane foam insulation, helping to retain maximum heat, which means you get hot water for a longer duration with less energy consumption.
  • Heating Element Compatibility: These tanks are fully compatible with electric, solar, or gas heating systems. Whether you prefer an eco-friendly solar system or opt for gas or electric heating, our tanks deliver optimal performance.
  • Temperature Control: Our storage tanks come with thermostatic control, allowing you to adjust the temperature to suit your needs.
  • Connections: Each tank features multiple inlet and outlet connections, providing greater flexibility for easy installation.
  • Pressure Rating: These tanks are rated for high-pressure applications up to 10 bar, ensuring reliable performance even in high-pressure systems.
  • Installation: Our storage tanks can be installed both vertically and horizontally, offering the best solution depending on your space and needs.
  • Safety Features: Each tank is equipped with safety valves and thermal expansion devices to prevent overheating and excessive pressure. These features ensure the safety of your system and provide additional protection against any potential accidents.
  • Customization Facility: We also offer complete customization for our customers. You can design the storage tank’s capacity, material, insulation, and other features according to your preferences, ensuring that it integrates perfectly with your system and helps in energy savings.
